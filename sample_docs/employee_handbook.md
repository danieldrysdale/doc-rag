# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Leave Entitlements

### Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave accrues from the first day of employment and may be taken after three months of continuous service. Unused annual leave does not expire but may not exceed 40 days at any time. Employees with more than 40 days accrued will be requested to schedule leave to bring the balance below this threshold.

### Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid personal/carer's leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding three consecutive days.

### Parental Leave
Primary carers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary carers are entitled to 4 weeks of paid parental leave. Parental leave may commence up to 6 weeks before the expected date of birth.

### Public Holidays
Employees are entitled to all NSW public holidays. Employees required to work on public holidays will receive double time and a half.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote work arrangements must be reviewed every 6 months. Employees working remotely are expected to be available during core hours of 10am–3pm AEST.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Maintain confidentiality of company and customer information
- Report conflicts of interest to their manager immediately
- Not engage in any form of harassment or discrimination

Breaches of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## IT and Equipment

Acme Corp provides a laptop, monitor, and peripherals to all permanent employees. Equipment remains the property of Acme Corp and must be returned on the last day of employment. Personal use of company equipment is permitted within reasonable limits, provided it does not compromise security or productivity.
