# Acme Corp — Refund and Returns Policy

## Overview

At Acme Corp, we stand behind everything we sell. This policy outlines your rights and our obligations regarding refunds, returns, and exchanges.

## Eligibility

Products are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided they are:
- In original, unused condition
- In original packaging with all accessories included
- Accompanied by proof of purchase (receipt or order number)

Software licenses and digital downloads are non-refundable once activated.

## How to Request a Refund

1. Contact our support team at support@acmecorp.com within the 30-day window.
2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
3. Our team will issue a Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) number within 2 business days.
4. Ship the item to our returns warehouse using the prepaid label we provide.
5. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping.

## Exchanges

Exchanges are processed as a return and new order. The original item must meet the standard return eligibility criteria above.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Perishable goods
- Hazardous materials
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Gift cards

## Contact

For all returns and refunds enquiries: support@acmecorp.com or call 1800-ACME-HELP Monday to Friday, 9am–5pm AEST.
